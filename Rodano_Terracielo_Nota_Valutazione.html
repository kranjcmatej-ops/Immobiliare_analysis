  • Rodano is a low-volume market — only ~14–15 transactions/year across all residential categories. Individual comparables carry more weight than trend lines.
  • Only 2–6 A02 (civil apartment) transactions per year — yearly averages are volatile and indicative only.
  • The overall market is stable to flat from 2023 to 2025 (avg +0.7%). No appreciation trend to factor in.
  • Size-discount pattern is robust and consistent: larger apartments (100+ m²) systematically trade at lower €/m² than smaller ones. This is the strongest and most reliable pattern in the dataset.
  • Of 17 active appartamenti listings on immobiliare.it (14 March 2026), the median ask is €2,255/m² and mean is €2,425/m². Option 2 at €3,072/m² is the 4th highest out of 17 — only above it are A+ new builds and a boutique renovated unit.
Context for the agent: These market figures are drawn from 44 registered transactions in the Agenzia delle Entrate database and 25 live listings captured on immobiliare.it on 14 March 2026. The flat three-year trend, combined with the size-discount pattern, means there is no market-supported rationale for Option 2 to be priced above the most recent same-complex transaction ceiling of ~€366,000.
